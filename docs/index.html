Saint Petersburg, Russia
July 13–22, 2021

Call for Participation

About the school

Focus

EMS-SCS Summer School will introduce participants to applications of experimental methods across social, behavioral and cognitive sciences while also providing training in a number of latest experimental and simulation tools and techniques.

Audience

The school aims at scholars from across a wide range of the aforementioned disciplines who are relatively experienced in research in their fields but might be novel to experimental methods and simulation. Advanced PhD students are also welcome.

Organizer

Laboratory for Social and Cognitive Informatics (SCILA) at the National Research University Higher School of Economics (HSE) in Saint Petersburg, Russia.

Participation

Dates

July 13-22, 2021.

See also our Schedule section.

Location and format

The school will be mostly online, but those students who are able and willing to be on site will have an opportunity to take classes in one of the HSE buildings in Saint Petersburg, Russia and to communicate informally between the classes. One of the workshops will be offline-only while online-only students will be able to take a different workshop at the same time.

How to apply?

To apply, please submit the following Google Form. We kindly ask you to attach your CV. After reviewing the applications, we will notify the participants about the acceptance.

Registration link will be sent to the participants via email after the reviewing process is finished.

Traveling

Unfortunately, due to the global pandemic of COVID-19, we cannot expect all of the participants to attend the School offline given the existing traveling restrictions. However, if you are able and willing to come, we will be pleased to welcome you at Higher School of Economics in Saint Petersburg.

Although we strongly hope that we will have more traveling opportunities by the time of our school, we cannot guarantee international participants a possibility to issue a visa on a short notice. Thus, if you need a visa to enter Russia but it might be challenging to get in on time, we advise you to participate online in order to avoid potential difficulties. We hope for your understanding and will answer any further questions regarding the possibility of your visit.

Organizers

The EMS-SCS Summer School 2021 is organized by Laboratory for Social and Cognitive Informatics (SCILA) which is based at the National Research University Higher School of Economics (HSE) in Saint Petersburg, Russia. SCILA is an interdisciplinary team of researchers that studies human interactions with information, technologies, and other humans relying on social, behavioral, cognitive, and computer sciences.
